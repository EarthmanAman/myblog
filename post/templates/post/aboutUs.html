About Us

How in Tech is a forum dedicated to educate the public on the complex aspect of technology in much simpler way . A way which is easy for everyone with no IT Knowledge to follow along. We were motivated by the fact that most tech blog tend to tackle technology in a complex way which discourage most of the users

We will also tackle complex problems in various areas not limited to programming, Artificial Intelligence and Cyber Security

We will also tackle the state of technology in Africa, its problems and achievement and the way forward. Africa has been in the tail end in many technological advancement

Our Mission

{#

#}

  • Educate and create awareness to the general public on various technology in a simple and easy way

  • Tackle problems in various category of technology

  • Highlight the state of technology in Africa
